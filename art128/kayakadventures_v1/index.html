ABOUT US

Aloha! We are a kayak tour company serving the eastside of Kauai. Come join us as we make our way down the historical Wailua River! You will not only get to see the scenic beauty of Kauai but you will also learn about the historical importance of Wailua. Join us, Kayak Adventures as we embark on this incredible journey!

Meet Felipe, he is the company owner! When he is not working on tours he keeps busy running two MMA schools, one in Sacremento and one here on Kauai. When he is not busy he stays active and enjoys many outdoor activies!

TOUR DETAILS

WHAT TO BRING

  • Comfortable and light clothes along with a swimsuit and hiking boots/sandals.
  • Sunscreen, hat, water, towel, sunglasses, rain gear and bug repellent.
  • An extra set of dry clothes for later and a camera to catch the beatuful scenery.

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS

  • Be physically capable to hike and paddle 4 miles as well as be comfortable in the water.
  • No bad knees, ankles, backs or recent surgeries.
  • Be at least 5 years of age.

WE PROVIDE

  • Double and single kayaks for those traveling alone.
  • Comfortable and supportive seating, backpacks to keep your personal items dry, life jackets and paddles.
  • A delicious deli lunch along with water and side of chips and cookies.

PREGNANCY

  • For your safety, we do not accept women who are 20 weeks pregnant or more on the tour date.

WEATHER

  • Unless there are extreme weather conditions such as flash floods, the tour will be scheduled rain or shine.

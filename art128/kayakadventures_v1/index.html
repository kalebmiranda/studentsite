ABOUT US

Aloha! We are a kayak tour company serving the eastside of Kauai. Come join us as we make our way down the historical Wailua River! You will not only get to see the scenic beauty of Kauai but you will also learn about the historical importance of Wailua. Join us, Kayak Adventures as we embark on this incredible journey!

Meet Felipe, he is the company owner! When he is not working on tours he keeps busy running two MMA schools, one in Sacremento and one here on Kauai. When he is not busy he stays active and enjoys many outdoor activies!
